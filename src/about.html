How to play

Spell as many words as possible within 2 minutes...

...on a jumbled-up keyboard!

Enjoy this fast-paced game of word recognition, memory and speed-thumbing abilities. Improve your vocabulary and spelling as you play, featuring over 500 words and counting.

Simply spell-out each word shown on screen. New words appear automatically after each word is correctly spelt. Incorrect letters are ignored. Optionally skip words by using the bottom right arrow key.
